What types of properties do you offer?

We offer a wide variety of residential properties, including houses, apartments, and luxury villas for sale. We also offer commercial properties for rent and investment opportunities.

How can I schedule a viewing of a property?

To schedule a viewing, you can simply visit the property details page and click on the "Schedule Viewing" button, or contact us directly through the contact page.

Do you offer financing options for property purchases?

Yes! We work closely with financial institutions to provide mortgage options for our clients. Please contact us for more details about financing options available.

How do I make an offer on a property?

Once you find a property you're interested in, you can submit your offer directly through the property page or by contacting one of our agents. Our team will guide you through the process from there.

What happens after my offer is accepted?

Once your offer is accepted, we will initiate the necessary paperwork and formalities for the purchase. Our team will assist you through each step of the process to ensure everything is completed smoothly.

Do you provide property management services?

Yes, we provide property management services for both buyers and investors. From finding tenants to maintaining the property, we ensure your investment is well taken care of.

How can I get in touch with an agent?

You can contact one of our agents directly through our contact page or by visiting our office. You can also send an email or call us, and we will respond to your inquiry promptly.

Can I list my property with BT Prime Estate?

Absolutely! We offer property listing services for sellers. Contact us for more details on how to list your property on our platform.
